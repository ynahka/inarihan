About the Farm Owner

Farm Owner

Jaime Esplana Badong

The owner and manager of INARIHAN FARM RESORT.
He is a man of action and love for the environment and nature. His vision is to make the earth livable for any life -plants, animals, human beings, and all living things. He is also committed and dedicated to protecting, preserving, beautifying, and making our nature even more fragrant for future generations

  • He was born on March 26, 1952, in Sta Cruz, Naga City.
  • He graduated at Camarines Sur Agricultural College [CSAC] in 1978.
  • Bachelor of Science in Agricultural Education major in Animal Husbandry in 1972-1973

Furthermore in August 1, 1978, he went to Guam and work as mechanic helper, service writer and later on manager of Honda Motors shop in 1985. He put up his own Auto Repair Shop until his retirement in 2014 as the owner and manager of the said shop.

At present, Mr. Jaime Esplana Badong , is passionately, running and managing his own INARIHAN FARM RESORT.
